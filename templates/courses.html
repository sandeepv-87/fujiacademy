Beginner Level

At the N5 level, learners gain a foundational understanding of Japanese, mastering basic grammar, around 800 words, and 100 kanji. They can participate in simple conversations, read and write short texts, and understand slow, clear speech in daily contexts.

N5 Course Outline
Conversational Level

At the N4 level builds on the basics, introducing intermediate grammar, about 1,500 words, and 300 kanji. Learners can handle everyday conversations, read simple texts, and understand standard-speed speech on familiar topics.

N4 Course Outline
Intermediate

At the N3, learners achieve intermediate proficiency, mastering complex grammar, 3,000 words, and 650 kanji. They can engage in detailed conversations, comprehend moderately complex texts, and understand standard-speed dialogues in various situations.
